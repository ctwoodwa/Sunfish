# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Sunfish is committed to being a welcoming project for everyone, regardless of
experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all project spaces (issues, PRs, Discussions)
- Give and receive feedback professionally
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people
- Assume good faith in others' contributions

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Sharing others' private information without consent

## Scope

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces: GitHub issues, pull requests,
Discussions, and any other official project channels.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported via
[GitHub's private vulnerability reporting](https://github.com/ctwoodwa/Sunfish/security/advisories/new)
or by contacting the maintainer directly through GitHub.

The maintainer will review reports promptly and take appropriate action, up to
and including permanent removal from the project.
